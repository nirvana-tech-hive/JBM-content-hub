🗺️ You can't automate what you don't understand.

Most automation projects fail not because the technology doesn't work, but because nobody actually knows how the process runs today.

Here's what happens when you skip process mapping:
→ You automate inefficiencies (making them faster)
→ Hidden steps derail your automation
→ Exceptions break your workflows
→ Workarounds circumvent your systems

Process mapping forces clarity:
• Who touches this process?
• What decisions are made?
• Where do handoffs occur?
• Why do exceptions exist?
• How long does each step take?
