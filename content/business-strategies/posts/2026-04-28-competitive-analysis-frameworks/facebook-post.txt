🎯 Most competitive analysis is superficial.

A glance at competitor websites. Occasional price comparisons. Anecdotal feedback. This leads to strategic errors, missed opportunities, and competitive surprises.

The right frameworks transform scattered observations into strategic insight.

In this guide:
→ Porter's Five Forces (the classic)
→ Competitive Positioning Matrix (2x2 mapping)
→ Feature Comparison Matrix (systematic scoring)
→ SWOT Analysis (per-competitor)
→ Win/Loss Analysis (most valuable data)
→ Competitor Monitoring System (ongoing)

Why competitive analysis matters:
✓ Strategic clarity for better decisions
✓ Opportunity identification from weaknesses
✓ Threat anticipation before disruption
✓ Customer understanding through comparison

Understand your market. Don't get blindsided.
