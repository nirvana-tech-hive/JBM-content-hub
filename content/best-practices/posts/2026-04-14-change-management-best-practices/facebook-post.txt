🔄 Why 70% of change initiatives fail (and how to be in the 30%)

Change isn't about better plans. It's about better execution on the human side.

Most organizations focus on:
• What's changing
• When it's changing
• How much it costs

They miss:
• WHO is affected and how
• WHY people resist
• WHAT emotions are triggered

The ADKAR model for individual change:
A - Awareness (why change is needed)
D - Desire (personal motivation to participate)
K - Knowledge (how to change)
A - Ability (capability to implement)
R - Reinforcement (support to sustain)

Everyone goes through these stages. Miss one, and change stalls.
