👥 The first 90 days determine everything.

Most onboarding is an afterthought—a few orientation sessions, some paperwork, then figure it out.

But effective onboarding:
→ Improves retention by 82%
→ Boosts productivity by 70%
→ Accelerates time-to-value dramatically

The four-phase framework:
1. PRE-BOARDING (Before Day 1)
   • Complete paperwork electronically
   • Set up all accounts and access
   • Send welcome package
   • Assign a buddy

2. ORIENTATION (Week 1)
   • Manager welcome meeting
   • Company context and culture
   • Role-specific training
   • Team introductions

3. INTEGRATION (Days 8-30)
   • Structured learning paths
   • Meaningful early projects
   • Relationship building
   • 30-day review

4. CONTRIBUTION (Days 31-90)
   • Increasing responsibility
   • Cultural integration
   • Performance development
   • 90-day comprehensive review

Remote onboarding requires extra intention—what office workers absorb organically must be explicitly designed.
