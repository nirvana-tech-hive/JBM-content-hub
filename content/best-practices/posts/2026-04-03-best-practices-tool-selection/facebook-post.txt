✅ The software you choose determines what you can build.

Most businesses select tools backwards:
❌ Start with features
❌ Compare based on pricing
❌ Decide without considering integration

The result? Tool sprawl, siloed data, and constant switching.

A better framework:
1. Define requirements first (what problems must it solve?)
2. Categorize features (must-have vs. nice-to-have)
3. Evaluate integration ecosystem (can it connect to what you already use?)
4. Consider total cost of ownership (implementation, training, maintenance)
5. Validate with hands-on testing
