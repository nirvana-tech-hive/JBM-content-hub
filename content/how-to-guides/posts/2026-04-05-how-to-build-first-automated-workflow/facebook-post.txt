🛠️ Your first automation shouldn't take weeks. It should take hours.

Most people overthink their first automation. They try to automate complex processes when simple wins build momentum.

Here's the step-by-step:
1. Choose your trigger (what starts the automation)
2. Select your actions (what happens automatically)
3. Connect your apps (Zapier, Make, or alternatives)
4. Test with real data
5. Monitor and refine
