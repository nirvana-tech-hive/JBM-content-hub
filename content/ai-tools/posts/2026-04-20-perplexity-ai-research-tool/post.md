## The Research Bottleneck

Every business decision requires research. What are competitors doing? What do customers want? What does the market look like? What are the regulatory requirements? The questions are endless, and traditional research is slow.

You open browser tabs. You search. You read. You synthesize. Hours disappear. And when you finally have answers, the information is often outdated or incomplete. The gap between needing knowledge and having it costs opportunities.

Perplexity AI transforms research from a time-consuming process into a real-time capability. This isn't just another search engine or chatbot—it's a research assistant that reads, synthesizes, and cites sources in seconds.

## Getting the Most from Perplexity

### Effective Prompting

**Be Specific:**
❌ "Tell me about marketing"
✅ "What are the most effective B2B marketing strategies for SaaS companies in 2026?"

**Request Format:**
❌ Open-ended questions
✅ "Provide a summary table comparing X, Y, and Z"

**Ask for Sources:**
❌ Accepting answers without verification
✅ "Include links to primary sources"

### Advanced Features

**Focus Mode:**
Narrow searches to specific sources:
- Academic papers only
- News sources only
- Reddit/forum discussions
- Specific websites

**Collections:**
Organize research into folders:
- Create project-specific collections
- Save threads for future reference
- Share collections with team

**Copilot:**
Interactive research assistant:
- Guides you through complex research
- Asks clarifying questions
- Provides deeper analysis
