## The Content Creation Bottleneck

## Key Features Deep Dive

### Brand Voice

The standout feature for businesses. Here's how it works:

**Step 1: Input Examples**
Upload 10-20 pieces of content that represent your ideal voice. Jasper analyzes patterns in word choice, sentence length, tone, and structure.

**Step 2: Define Parameters**
Specify your brand characteristics:
- Formal vs. conversational
- Technical vs. accessible
- Authoritative vs. friendly
- Professional vs. playful

**Step 3: Generate and Refine**
Create content in your brand voice. The output matches your style without explicit prompting every time.

**Real Impact:** Companies report 70% reduction in editing time when using Brand Voice consistently.
