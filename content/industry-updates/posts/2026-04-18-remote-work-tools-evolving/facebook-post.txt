=== PART 2: Solution & Story (Post separately) ===

Here's what the latest industry developments reveal:

PLATFORM CONSOLIDATION is real.
Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom, Google Workspace—they're all expanding to become comprehensive platforms.

Before adding another point solution, ask: "Does my existing platform already do this?"

AI INTEGRATION is everywhere.
Every major platform now includes:
• Meeting transcription and summarization
• Real-time translation and captions
• Writing assistance
• Smart scheduling
• Automated action item extraction

Reassess your tech stack. Tools you pay for separately may now be included in platforms you already use.

ASYNC-FIRST tools are rising.
Remote doesn't mean "always online."

Loom for video messaging. Notion for async documentation. Miro for collaborative visual work. Figma for design across time zones.

The question isn't "What meetings should we have?"
It's "Could this be async?"

But here's the critical insight:

When evaluating tools, use the Seven Criteria:
1. Integration capability
2. Async support
3. AI features
4. Security and compliance
5. Scalability
6. User experience
7. Vendor viability

Which of these are you ignoring?
