## GUIDELINES:

### Length: 40-60 words (short but valuable)

### Structure:
1. Opening hook with emoji (1 line)
2. Link on its own line
3. Value bullets (3-4 points)
4. Social CTA (1 line)

### Opening Options:
- "📚 Here's your complete guide to..."
- "📚 The full breakdown is here:"
- "📚 Get the complete resource:"
- "📚 Your [topic] guide is ready:"

### Bullet Point Style:
- Use • (bullet) for bullets
- Keep each bullet short (5-8 words)
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- Create curiosity, don't reveal everything

### Closing CTAs:
- "Save this post and share with someone who needs this! 🔄"
- "Save this for later and tag a friend! 🚀"
- "Bookmark this for your next planning session! 💡"
- "Share with your team! 👥"
