# FACEBOOK POST TEMPLATE

[EMOJI] [COMPELLING HOOK - Bold statement or question that stops the scroll]

[Supporting statistic, fact, or context that amplifies the problem]

[Emotional or logical expansion on why this matters]

[What if / Imagine statement that creates desire for solution]

In this guide, I break down:
→ [Key benefit 1]
→ [Key benefit 2]
→ [Key benefit 3]
→ [Key benefit 4]

[Closing statement that creates urgency or curiosity]

👇 Check the comments for the full guide

---

## GUIDELINES:

### Length: 100-150 words (optimal for engagement)

### Structure:
1. Hook (1-2 lines) - Stop the scroll
2. Context (2-3 lines) - Why it matters
3. Value preview (3-5 bullet points) - What they'll learn
4. CTA (1 line) - Direct to comments

### Formatting:
- Use line breaks between each section
- Use → for bullet points (not •)
- Use 2-4 emojis strategically
- NEVER include links

### Hook Formulas:
- "The biggest [misconception] about [topic]..."
- "[X]% of [people/businesses] get [topic] wrong..."
- "Your [pain point] isn't about [surface cause]..."
- "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]..."
- "[Statement that challenges common belief]..."

### CTA Options:
- "👇 Full guide in the comments"
- "Check the comments for the complete breakdown 👇"
- "Link in comments 👇"
- "Details in comments 👇"
